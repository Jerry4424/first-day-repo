Chicken Fried Rice

Chicken fried rice is a mouthwatering fusion of tender, seasoned chicken pieces tossed with fragrant jasmine rice, colorful vegetables, and a perfect balance of soy sauce and savory spices. Each bite delivers a comforting blend of smoky wok flavor, hearty textures, and a satisfying mix of flavors that will leave you craving more. Perfect for a quick, wholesome, and utterly delicious meal!

Here’s a list of ingredients for a classic chicken fried rice:

  1. Main ingredients
    • Cooked rice (preferably day-old for better texture)
    • Chicken (diced or shredded; can be breast or thigh)
    • Eggs (scrambled lightly)
  2. Vegetables
    • Carrots (diced)
    • Peas
    • Green onions (chopped)
    • Garlic (minced)
    • Optional: Onion (diced) or bell peppers
  3. Seasonings & Sauces
    • Soy sauce (for savory depth)
    • Oyster sauce (optional, for richness)
    • Sesame oil (for nutty aroma)
    • Garlic (minced)
    • Salt and pepper (to taste)
    • Optional: chili flakes
  4. Cooking Oil
    • Vegetable oil or canola oil (for stir-frying)
    • Oyster sauce (optional, for richness)
    • Sesame oil (for nutty aroma)
    • Garlic (minced)
    • Salt and pepper (to taste)
    • Optional: fish sauce (for flavor complexity)

Here is the recipe for our Chicken Fried Rice:

Ingredients

  • cups cooked rice (preferably day-old, chilled)
  • cup chicken (diced or shredded; cooked or raw)
  • 2 large eggs
  • ½ cup carrots (diced)
  • ½ cup peas (frozen or fresh)
  • 2 green onions (chopped, white and green parts separated)
  • 2 garlic cloves (minced)
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce (optional)
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • Salt and pepper (to taste)
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil (for stir-frying)
  1. Prepare the Ingredients
    • Ensure 2 cups of rice are cooked and chilled for better texture.
    • Dice or shred 1 cup of chicken and chop the vegetables.
  2. Cook the Chicken
    • Heat 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil in a large wok or skillet over medium-high heat.
    • Add 1 cup of chicken (if raw) and stir-fry until fully cooked and browned (about 4–5 minutes).
      If pre-cooked, stir-fry for 1–2 minutes to warm through. Remove the chicken from the pan and set aside.
  3. Scramble the Eggs
    • In the same pan, add another ½ tablespoon of vegetable oil if needed. Crack 2 large eggs directly into
      the pan and scramble until just set (1–2 minutes). Remove and set aside with the chicken.
  4. Sauté the Vegetables
    • Add the remaining ½ tablespoon of vegetable oil to the pan. Sauté 2 minced garlic cloves for about 30 seconds until fragrant.
    • Add ½ cup diced carrots and ½ cup peas, cooking for 2–3 minutes until softened
  5. Combine the Rice and Ingredients
    • Push the vegetables to one side of the pan and add the 2 cups of rice. Break up any clumps using a spatula and stir everything together.
    • Return the cooked chicken and scrambled eggs to the pan, mixing thoroughly.
  6. Season the Fried Rice
    • Drizzle 3 tablespoons of soy sauce, 1 tablespoon of oyster sauce (if using), and 1 teaspoon of
      sesame oil over the mixture. Stir-fry for 2–3 minutes, ensuring everything is well-coated and heated through.
    • Taste and adjust seasoning with salt and pepper as needed.
  7. Finish and Serve
    • Sprinkle the chopped green onion tops (2 green onions) over the fried rice. Serve hot and enjoy!
