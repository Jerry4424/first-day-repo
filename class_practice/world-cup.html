World Cup News

“For Indonesians, football is life,” says Marselino Ferdinan when asked to describe the passion for the sport in the southeast Asian country. “It’s like Brazil. The people are just crazy about football.”

The 20-year-old forward sent those feverish fans into ecstasy when he scored a brilliant brace against Saudi Arabia in their most recent FIFA World Cup 26™ qualifier. His goals earned Indonesia a stunning 2-0 win and ignited their hopes of reaching next year’s global showpiece.

Viewed as one of the nation’s most promising talents even before that famous night in Jakarta, Ferdinan’s heroics have sent him to altogether new levels of stardom.
